About
At Entrepreneurship cell NITH, we believe in bridging the gap between who we are and who we want to be. Our prime objective is to help the students who are eager to become the entrepreneurs of future in developing successful business ideas and hence transmuting them into their physical form.With just the right amount of aspiration,perseverance and execution, we hope to transform the individuals with creative minds from a set of "job seekers" to "job creators".
Vision
E-CELL NITH has the vision to create an entrepreneurial ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs and promote entrepreneurial growth in NITH. We aim to create a place where students can access entrepreneurial resources, network with community entrepreneurs, and share ideas. We are here to nurture, refine, incubate as well as facilitate nascent business ventures from NITH
Mission
Entrepreneurship and Business Development in Himachal region in our alma-mater, National Institute of Technology Hamirpur (H.P.). We aim to reshape our annual financial structure to make it ‘THE’ college Start-up Accelerator platform for anyone involved in or with Entrepreneurship and Business Development in North India.

STARTUP INTERNSHIP PROGRAM 2020

Start-up Internship Program (SIP) which is designed to meet the demand of students who are excited, engaged and curious to know more about the start-up community and are thinking about pursuing a career in the start-up space. Through the SIP, interns can take part in educational programming to help explore the possibilities in the start-up industry.

After the CVs are uploaded at the time of registration, all the registered candidates have to take a General Aptitude Test. This would be followed by a Technical test as per the instructions of the company. The shortlisted candidates would have an interview session with the respective company on the designated date over Skype. The selected candidates would be announced by the company itself and the results will be uploaded on our website.

Visit our official site at ecellnith.org. Click on the 'Startup Internship Program (SIP)' button which leads you to the Registration page. After going through the company list, click on the 'Register' button. Fill in your details and upload your CV and click 'Submit'. A confirmation mail will be sent to you afterwards.

The following points does not necessarily define that you will only be accessed on the basis of this, rather meeting these criterias gives you an edge over the other candidates. 1. A good score in General Aptitude Test and Technical Test. 2. Major projects undertaken by you. 3. A good CGPI
